Getting Started

Ready to wake your devices? Ensure they support Wake-on-LAN and have it enabled in their BIOS/UEFI settings. Your network adapter might also need specific configuration.

Our application simplifies sending the necessary 'magic packets', but proper device and network setup is key. Need detailed instructions? Check out our Wiki for setup guides and troubleshooting tips.
