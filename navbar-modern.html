Modern Navigation with Slide Panels

This approach completely eliminates dropdown issues! Instead of traditional dropdowns, we use slide-out panels that come from the side. This is how modern apps like Notion, Linear, and Slack handle navigation.

Benefits:

  • Glass effect works perfectly on both navbar and panels
  • More space for content and descriptions
  • Better mobile experience
  • Modern, app-like feel
  • No z-index or backdrop-filter conflicts
  • Smooth animations and transitions
